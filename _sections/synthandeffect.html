ParanoidChorus

A chorus-like effect based on JUCE [repo]

An effect you can use either as a plug-in or a standalone application. Knobs on the top-left are basically parameters you can modify on a delay/chorus/flanger effect. In "mood" selection you can control the level of modulating, and in "mode" selection you can choose different oscillators. Plus, the "mod" slider works as a powerful magic booster for the LFO, generating awesome sounds from whatever input.

shakeFM

An OSC-based remote control FM synthesizer based on Wekinator [repo]

Parameters of FM synthesizer are controlled by accelerators built in the phones via machine learning algorithms. Neural network and polynomial regression were applied while training the model. Thus, subtle gesture change of your hand holding the phone will generate a new tone in the synthesizer.
Shake it hard, and you will get the best damn sound.

GrainBeats

A Drum Machine with a Digital Clock Modulation to Create Complex Groovy Rhythm Patterns [repo]

The "Bad Timing!!!" mode: the digital clock triggering the beats will be modulated corresponding to the parameters below. Plus, you can modify the modulating frequency, modulating amount, and you can even give the modulation a swing. You can bang the drums directly hit the "BANG!!!" button, it triggers the beats cooperating with modulation.
