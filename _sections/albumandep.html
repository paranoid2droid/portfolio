The Artifacts of Ripples: A personal music project of mine, exploring the edge of making music in a bedroom. I made the album Radio Rex and other EPs totally by iPad, getting rid of the complexity and expensiveness of traditional music production in studio.

Subaqua Roaming Guide: A band with my friends Shenyang Xu and Yonglin Zhang. I did some parts of the composing, recording, sampling, mixing, vocal, electric guitar, synthesizer, and sound effects in the works.
